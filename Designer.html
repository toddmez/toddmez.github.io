Teaching Examples

Here are some examples of assignments I would give during a course. These tutorials would be generated and improved over time. Depending on the feedback that I would receive from students I would either add or modify the instructions.

After a few courses the assignments became the basis for teaching specific skills to the students that would need in order to complete a final project. At Purdue and Ivy Tech it was a project of their choice. A 3D Charcter Model, 3D Architectural Visualization or an Animated Mechanical Assembly. In all cases I would have personal examples to show the students of the work that either I did myself or previous students work. This would help give me a connection to the students and inspire them to take it upon themselves to create something original that challenged them

Learning Interactive Examples

Muscle Fibers

An interactive for an Anatomy and Physiology course. The format of this activity having tabs lended itself to a simpler way for the student to navigate the lesson. This activity ended up in a Rise presentation that included knowledge checks as well as other learning content.
